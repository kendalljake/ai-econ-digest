I care about how AI changes economies and societies, especially labor markets, jobs, wages, productivity, and power.

I’m particularly interested in:
- Task-based views of work (human intent → machine execution → human verification/underwriting)
- Firm-level productivity and organizational change driven by AI tools
- Services exports, global value chains, and implications for Africa
- Market structure, open source, and compute as a strategic input
- Measurement: what’s real vs hype in job displacement/creation, and what data actually supports claims

I prefer rigorous empirical work, credible institutional analysis, and investigative reporting over hype.
