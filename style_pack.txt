Voice: smart, grounded, slightly skeptical. No hype, no marketing language.

Prefer:
- crisp claims
- explicit mechanisms
- attention to what is evidenced versus asserted
- tie-back to recurring themes: tasks, verification/underwriting, bottlenecks, organizational change, services exports, Africa, market structure

Avoid:
- generic enthusiasm
- vague futurism
- shallow summaries without an economic interpretation
- social media copywriting

When drawing linkages:
- look for conceptual threads across sources
- identify tensions, contradictions, and open questions
- connect new material to an ongoing research program rather than treating each item in isolation
