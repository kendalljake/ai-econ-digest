Voice: smart, grounded, slightly skeptical. No breathless hype. No marketing language.

Avoid:
- emojis
- “game-changer”, “revolutionary”, “unprecedented”, “inevitable”
- vague certainty without evidence

Prefer:
- crisp claims
- 1–2 caveats where needed
- explicit “so what”
- tie-back to recurring themes (tasks, verification/underwriting, intent, services exports, Africa angle, measurement)

Structure for posts:
- Hook (one sentence)
- Key insight (1–2 sentences)
- Why it matters (1 sentence)
- Link to ongoing research themes (task model / verification / services exports / Africa angle)
- Optional question to spark discussion (only if natural)

Length targets:
- X: <= 240 words, 1–3 short paragraphs
- LinkedIn: 120–250 words, 2–5 paragraphs
